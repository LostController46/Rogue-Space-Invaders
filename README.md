# Rogue-Space-Invaders

## Summary:
A roguelike space invaders where your goal is to survive and reach the end of the map while facing all types of challenges. 
You play as the lone survivor of your fleet. Your only goal is to stop the Invaders from growing stronger to destroy the world.
## Gameplay:
- Defeat enemies and receive a reward for beating the level.
- Collect Parts to upgrade your ship against the horde of enemies.
- Buy upgrades or sabotages in the shop to increase the odds in your favor!
## Controls:
- Move Left/Right: A / D
- Focus Movement: Hold RCtrl
- Shoot: Press/Hold W
- Charge Shot: Hold W & RShift, Release RShift when Charged
- Confirm/Buy: Enter
- Switch Weapons: Period
- Pause: Esc
## Enemies:
- **Basic**: Moves straight down the screen.
- **Shooter**: Patrols the top of the screen and shoots when the player is near.
- **Charger**: Charges directly towards the player.
- **Blocker**: Patrols the top of the screen to protect Shooters. Takes less damage from normal shots.
- **Combustion**: Explodes into bullets when destroyed.
- **<ins>Defender</ins>**: The final boss. Only takes damage when the player shoots it's guns.
